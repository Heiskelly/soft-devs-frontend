Basic document flow

I am a basic block level element. My adjacent block level element sit on new lines me.

By default we span 100% of the width of our parent element, and our height is as tall as our child content. Our total width and height is our content + padding + border width/height.

We are seperated by our margins. Because of margin collapsing, We are seperated by the width of one of our margins, not both.
